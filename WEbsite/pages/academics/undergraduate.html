List of Documents required for registration IAAU
1.    Original High School Diploma of Graduation (including high school transcript).
2.    Original “ORT” certificate.
3.    Copy of ID Card.
4.    Copy of Military Registration Certificate (boys only)
5.    ID Photos: size 3x4 – (4 pieces).
6.    Initial Payment of 30% from total Tuition Fee.
